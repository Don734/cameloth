Welcome Cameloth Group

We Provide Your Future

Since 2010 Cameloth Group has been a distributor of home appliances, electronics, medical equipment and fast-moving consumer goods of the leading global brands. Our extensive knowledge of the market has helped us to build and develop long-term partnership agreements with our customers from a diverse background globally.

We at Cameloth Group have always been committed to delivering outstanding products and services to our customers in MENA region, Caucasus and Central Asia. Our Company is constantly introducing new products for our customers promoting new businesses and strengthening ties between manufacturers, distributors and buyers from across the region and ensuring direct access to international brands.

Cameloth Group always does the best possible and aims to create long lasting, successful and profitable partnership with the clients providing the highest level of professionalism and operational excellence
